World's Best Lasagna

If you’ve never made lasagna before or haven’t found a good recipe, you have come to the right place. Invite a friend because you won’t want to keep this dish to yourself!

Here's what you will need to put together this recipe

  • 1 pound italian sausage
  • Lasagna pasta
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. In a pot add all the ingredients for the sauce
  2. In a baking tray place lasagna noodles then pour sauce then cheese
  3. Once finished top it with cheese
  4. Set in the oven for 30 mins at 375 degrees
